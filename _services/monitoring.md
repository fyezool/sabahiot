## Remote monitoring systems alert you of threats to your equipment or facilities

SabahIOT offer 24/7 monitoring by using data from HVAC components such as
1. Pressure
2. Compressor Oil
3. Condensing Fan
4. Sound Analysis
5. Fire Damper
6. Vibration Analysis
